Explosive Squat Jumps

Lower Body Power & Plyometrics

timer 45s local_fire_department ~120 kcal speed High Intensity

Target Areas

  • Quadriceps
  • Glutes
  • Calves
  • Core (Stabilization)

tips_and_updates Form Focus

Maintain a neutral spine throughout the movement. Do not let your knees collapse inward during the landing phase. Focus on max height over speed.

warning

Avoid if you have acute knee or lower back issues. Substitute with standard air squats if needed.

Execution Sequence

1

Setup & Stance

Stand with feet slightly wider than shoulder-width apart. Point toes outward slightly. Keep chest tall and brace your core tight.

2

The Loading Phase

Hinge at the hips and bend your knees to lower into a deep squat. Swing your arms back to gather momentum.

3

Explosive Launch

Drive violently through your heels, extending hips, knees, and ankles simultaneously. Throw arms upward to maximize vertical height.

4

Soft Impact

Land on the mid-foot to balls of your feet, immediately absorbing the impact by sinking smoothly back into the next squat position.
